# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Email **jmsernatuya@gmail.com** with subject: `[SECURITY] Mental Health Specialists in Ibero-America`

Do NOT open public issues for security vulnerabilities.

We will acknowledge within 48 hours and respond fully within 7 days.

## Scope

- Data integrity issues that could lead to misleading research conclusions
- Privacy violations in any linked data
- Malicious content in dependencies
